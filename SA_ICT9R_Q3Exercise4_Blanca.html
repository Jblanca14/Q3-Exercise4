Bicol

Located in the southern part of luzon, BIcol is a great place to visit during the summer with many tourist spots and is famous for their spicy foods and welcoming people

While at bicol you can visit one of the many beaches that are there the most popular one being Gota Beach with its white sand and clear, but if beaches arent your thing but you still want to go swimming you can visit Aqua Crib a swimming resort perfect to take your friends or family.
